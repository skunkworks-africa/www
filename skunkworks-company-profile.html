Company Overview

Skunkworks (Pty) Ltd is a South African–based technology consulting, systems integration, and training company delivering enterprise-grade ICT solutions to public and private sector clients. We specialize in cloud and hybrid infrastructure, cybersecurity, AI-driven automation, custom software development, and vendor-authorized technical training.

Our approach combines deep technical expertise with practical business alignment, enabling clients to modernize systems, secure digital assets, and scale operations efficiently.
